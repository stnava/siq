## Key Findings

1. **LDBPN Consistent Dominance**: LDBPN outperforms bilinear interpolation and feed-forward models across all geometric and organic classes, demonstrating the robustness of back-projection units.
2. **Complex vs Simple Geometry**: Models generally perform better on simple geometric phantoms and layered structures than on fractal noise or vessel tubes, which contain higher frequency details.
3. **PixelShuffle vs Resize-Conv**: The Resize-Conv models (ESPCN-RC, WDSR-RC) show slightly lower PSNR compared to their PixelShuffle counterparts, consistent with results on MRI crops, showing that the checkerboard mitigation path has a slight capacity trade-off across different domain geometries.
